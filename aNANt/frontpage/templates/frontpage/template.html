About

aNANt is an initiative of Materials Theory and Simulations Group, Materials Research Center, Indian Institute of Science Bangalore to develop and host an open-access online repository of functional materials. aNANt database shares the structures and electronic properties of computationally designed two-dimensional layered materials in a single platform.

Developed by Materials Theory and Simulations Group, Indian Institute of Science
