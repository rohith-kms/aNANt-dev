Welcome to the MXene database.

MXenes are a class of two-dimensional inorganic compounds. These materials consist of few atoms thick layers of transition metal carbides, nitrides, or carbonitrides. MXenes combine metallic conductivity of transition metal carbides and hydrophilic nature.

Search for MXenes here

MXenes have M1 & M2 transition elements, X Carbon/Nitrogen and T1 and T2 functional groups.

Calculation details: Structure optimization and band gap calculation of the bb’ phase of MXene are performed by density functional theory (DFT) using the Vienna ab-initio simulation package (VASP). Electronic exchange correlations are treated by the Perdew-Burke-Ernzerhof (PBE) parameterization of GGA, and core-valance electron interactions are represented by projector augmented wave (PAW) potentials. Optimized plane wave basis set, fine k-grid and converged force criteria are used to optimize the structures.
